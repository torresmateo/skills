---
name: confirm-search
description: Double-check that articles from the Internet are based on credible facts and not only self-promoting
---


1. After a Web Search, reason about why a specific decision was made and check for self-promotion. Especially when choosing a vendor or service.

- Is the vendor the main source of information? If so, look for third-party revies
- Are the articles used to make this decision promotional comparisons without real evidence? If so, investigate the listed comptetitors at a deeper level
- Are claims about one vendor coming from another vendor? If so, please verify the claims

2. When the reasoning is done, make the following assessment

- Is the information about all options relevant to make the decision? What's missing? Search for it
- For coding tools and services, assess:
  - Which one offers the best developer experience. 
  - Which one offers the clearest documentation.
  - Which one focuses on established security best practices
